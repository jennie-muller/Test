During my career I have been lucky enough to work with creative projects. I have turned my hobbies and interests into events and festivals. Another important learning is also to be open to learn from others and welcoming feedback. My journey into UX-design is simply because I want to take a step closer to understand "why" and "what" I can do to make the correlation between customer and product easier, smoother, and more fun! I believe that the customer is the core for each service or product and to improve a product or service you must first understand the customer. With creative thinking, motivation and curiosity I believe anything is possible.
