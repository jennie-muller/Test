Case Studies

My approach

Get into the users mindset

Step 1

Start Online

Find out as much as I can about
the subject trough published
articles,and other relevant
sources.

Step 2

Competitive analysis

Find out what the current product
offers. Compare this to what
competitors offer.

Step 3

Gather questions

List all of the questions that I
have based on research and
decide which ones are most
important to pursue.

Step 4

Candidate for interview

This may include creating,
posting, and gathering
information from a survey. The
survey results can then be used
in persona generation.

Step 5

Interview

The lynch pin of the research
process - as well as a great idea
generator.

Step 6

Design studio

A fun way of generating creative
ideas to solve the user's problem
- used to create the first
prototype

Step 7

Prototype and test

Prototype, test, revise - repeat
until an intuitive design emerges
that is what the user needs. To
maintain a rapid, agile process, I
would start with low-fidelity
prototypes.

Step 8

Wireframes and mockups

My passion for research - and to
understand the users why, allow
me to find creativity with design
to create work from briefs to
mockups.
